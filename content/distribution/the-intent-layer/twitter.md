## Post 1

React made frontend work declarative by moving us above manual DOM mutation.

---

## Post 2

I think AI engineering is going through a similar shift.

But it is not just about prompts.

It is prompts + specs + validation loops.

---

## Post 3

The useful artifact is not:
"build me a dashboard"

It is a declaration of:

- behavior
- constraints
- scenarios
- boundaries
- proof

---

## Post 4

Without validation, the declaration is just intent.

With validation, the agent can compare the running product against the spec and keep iterating until reality matches.

---

## Post 5

Prompts start the interaction.
Specs stabilize the intent.
Validation closes the loop.

That is the abstraction shift.
