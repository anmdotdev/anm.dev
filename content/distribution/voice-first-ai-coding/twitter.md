## Post 1

Voice-first AI coding is not really about speed.

It is about context.

I type fast.
But typing makes me compress requests too aggressively.

---

## Post 2

When I type a prompt, I tend to omit:

- file paths
- constraints
- edge cases
- "do not break this" warnings

Not because I do not know them.
Because typing all of that feels expensive.

---

## Post 3

Voice changes the economics.

Saying one more sentence is cheap.
So the model gets more of the context already in your head.

That usually matters more than polished phrasing ever will.

---

## Post 4

That is why voice-first works for AI coding:

not because speaking is faster,
but because it makes thoroughness easy enough to do every time.
