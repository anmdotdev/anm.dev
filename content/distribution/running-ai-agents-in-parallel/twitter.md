## Post 1

Running 3 AI agents in parallel feels powerful.

But parallelism is not the first trick.
It is the last one.

---

## Post 2

If your single-agent loop is weak, more agents just multiply the chaos.

Parallel work only helps after:

- strong prompts
- bounded tasks
- good feedback loops
- disciplined review

---

## Post 3

The real bottleneck is usually not the models.

It is you:

- framing work
- answering questions
- reviewing output
- merging decisions

---

## Post 4

That is why I think multi-agent workflows are a reward for discipline, not a shortcut around it.

When the foundation is solid, the leverage is real.
